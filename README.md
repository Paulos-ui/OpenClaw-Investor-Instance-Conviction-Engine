# OpenClaw Conviction Engine

OpenClaw Conviction Engine is a dedicated AI instance for crypto investors that captures investment theses, stress-tests assumptions using on-chain and market data, highlights invalidation conditions, and records conviction over time. Designed for **non-technical investors**, it delivers decision support without nodes, code, or complex setup, turning thinking into **auditable, repeatable infrastructure**.

## Overview

The Conviction Engine transforms how crypto investors make decisions:

- Capture and formalize investment theses  
- Stress-test assumptions with AI using on-chain and market data  
- Track invalidation conditions  
- Maintain a **Conviction Ledger** for reflection and audit  
- Designed to scale for Spark community users  

It’s open-source, transparent, and community-aligned.

---

## Features

- ✅ Dedicated OpenClaw AI agent per investor  
- ✅ Conviction Ledger (timestamped, auditable, optional on-chain proof)  
- ✅ Decision support for non-technical users  
- ✅ Fully open-source with community-first design  
- ✅ Easy integration for Spark Hackathon participants

## Usage

1. Initialize your AI instance  
2. Enter your investment thesis  
3. Run AI stress-test on your assumptions  
4. Record conviction and view the Conviction Ledger  
5. Optional: store a hash on-chain for proof
